Our mission
Our mission is to revive and preserve the essence of Bhartiya Khel, ensuring these traditional games remain a cherished part of our culture. We aim to promote their significance, foster community engagement, and pass their legacy to future generations for a deeper connection with our heritage.
The need to preserve Bhartiya Khel lies in their role in protecting India’s cultural heritage and uniting communities. These games teach valuable life skills, encourage creativity, and build connections across generations. They offer a platform to celebrate diversity and ensure that traditional sports remain relevant and cherished in today’s world.

Our Games

Gilli Danda

Gilli Danda is a game where players hit a small stick (gilli) with a larger stick (danda) and score points by striking and launching the gilli as far as possible.

Kho Kho

Kho Kho is a game where players chase opponents, tag them, and dodge within a rectangular field, with one team defending and the other attacking.

Kabadi

Kabaddi is a game where players raid opponents, tag defenders, and return while holding their breath and avoiding tackles.

Lagori

Lagori is a game where players stack flat stones, knock them down with a ball, and try to rebuild the stack while avoiding being hit by the opposing team.
